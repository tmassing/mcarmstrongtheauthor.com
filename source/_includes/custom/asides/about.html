About Me

M. C. Armstrong recently embedded with SEAL Team 4 in Haditha, Iraq. He published extensively on the Iraq war through The Winchester Star. He is the winner of a Pushcart Prize in fiction. His work has appeared in The Missouri Review, The Gettysburg Review, Dead Letters, Kearney Street Books, Mayday, and other journals and anthologies. He is the co-founder and co-editor of Convergence Review. He is the lead guitarist and vocalist for Viva la Muerte.
